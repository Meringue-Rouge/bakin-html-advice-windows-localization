Camera Tool

This screen specifies the camera to be used in the game. The camera movements created here are used in the game by specifying them from events, etc.

Cameras are divided into two main categories: [Battle Cameras] and [Cameras Used on Maps].

The [Battle Camera] is the camera that will be used for each scene during the battle. Unless otherwise specified in the event, the camera specified here will be used.

The [Camera Used on Maps] is used as the camera in operation or the camera used for events.

Move the camera with the mouse while looking at the [Preview] to determine the camera's position. Add [frames] to the [Timeline] under [Preview], decide the camera position for each frame and the time it takes to move to the next frame, and then press the play button.

Try using this camera that lets you move freely in 3D space!
