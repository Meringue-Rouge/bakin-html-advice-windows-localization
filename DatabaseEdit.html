This section allows you to specify the ability values and in-game behavior of the cast members, items, and classes that appear in the game. (In this tool, game characters and monsters with ability values are called [Casts].)

After setting, click the [Test Play] button at the top of the Map Editor screen to start the game, and the main cast you just specified should appear on the screen as [Operating Cast Member].
